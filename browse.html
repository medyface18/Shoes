Patronus' features and how to use them

How to use Patronus

Upon creating an account, users' responses to the forms will be stored in a database. Registration consists of four steps, including the user's first and last name, email address and phone number, birthdate, username, and password. After signing in, users can create pods by inputting the names and grade of their fellow classmates/groupmates into a form. If students wish to travel to each other's houses, mainting social distancing, of course, everyone will be required to log their temperatures. The entire pod's temperatures will be put on display on the dashboard. Additionally, students can deliver information amongst each other by writing the main class focus. Users can also fill out a to-do form for students to log the activities they need to finish. All the data will be stored and saved.

Why use Patronus?

According to the NY Times, pods are in demand and there is no safe way to create them. With Patronus we enable a way for families of all background to create in person or online pods that help guranteee safety with temperature tracking and help find resources such as tutors and online help! Patronus is motivational and adaptable to any educational needs that students may have and bridges the gaps causes by distance learning.
